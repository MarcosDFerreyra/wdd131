Places Nearby

El Preferido de Palermo – Traditional Argentine Cuisine

Famous for its classic Buenos Aires dishes, excellent steak, and vibrant pink building.

Distance: 10–15 min by car

Cabaña Las Lilas – Premium Steakhouse

High-end parrilla with top-quality meats and waterfront views.

Distance: 5–7 min

Havanna Café (Florida Street) – Coffee & Snacks

Quick coffee, pastries, alfajores, and light meals.

Distance: 5 min walking

La Biela – Historic Café in Recoleta

Iconic café next to Recoleta Cemetery.

Distance: 10 min by car / 20 min walking

Museo Nacional de Bellas Artes

Argentina’s most important art museum. Free entrance.

Distance: 10–12 min by car

Museo de Arte Latinoamericano (MALBA)

Modern and contemporary Latin-American art.

Distance: 12–15 min by car

Centro Cultural Kirchner (CCK)

Massive cultural center with concerts, exhibitions and free activities.

Distance: 7 min walking

Teatro Colón

One of the world’s most famous opera houses. Guided tours available.

Distance: 10 min walking

Galerías Pacífico Shopping Mall

High-end mall with fashion brands, restaurants and artistic murals.

Distance: 6–8 min walking

Florida Street (Calle Florida)

Pedestrian street full of shops, cafés and souvenirs.

Distance: 5 min walking

Patio Bullrich (Recoleta)

Luxury shopping with premium international brands.

Distance: 10 min by car

Recoleta Mall

Shops, cinema, food court and a beautiful neighborhood.

Distance: 10 min by car / 20 min walking

Plaza San Martín (Retiro)

Historic park with monuments and views of Torre Monumental.

Distance: 3 min walking

Puerto Madero Waterfront

Boardwalk, restaurants, modern architecture and sunset views.

Distance: 10–12 min walking

Costanera Sur Ecological Reserve

Huge natural reserve ideal for walking and cycling.

Distance: 20 min walking from Puerto Madero

Obelisco de Buenos Aires

The most iconic monument in the city.

Distance: 15 min walking

Casa Rosada (Government House)

Historic building with guided tours on weekends.

Distance: 10–12 min walking

Cementerio de Recoleta

Famous for its mausoleums, art and history.

Distance: 20 min walking / 10 min by car

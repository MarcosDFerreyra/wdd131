About the Hotel

Located in the heart of the elegant Recoleta neighborhood, BS Hotel City blends modern comfort, personalized service, and an unbeatable location to experience the best of Buenos Aires. Our hotel was created with the mission of offering a warm, sophisticated, and accessible stay for travelers from around the world. We stand out for our modern and relaxed atmosphere, designed to provide the perfect rest after exploring the city. Just steps away from museums, parks, historic cafés, cultural centers, and many of Buenos Aires’ main attractions, we are the ideal starting point for both leisure and business travelers. At BS Hotel City, we believe every detail matters. That’s why we offer comfortable rooms, carefully designed spaces, dedicated service, and the peace of mind of staying in one of the city’s safest and most prestigious areas. More than just a place to stay, we are a space where every guest can feel at home while experiencing an authentic and memorable Buenos Aires visit.
